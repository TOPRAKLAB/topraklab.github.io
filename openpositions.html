Postdoc positions available

Postdoctoral Fellows

As of August 2014, we have three postdoctoral research fellow positions available in our group. Candidates with training in bacteria and/or yeast genetics, biochemistry, computational biology, bioinformatics, microfluidics, and mathematical modeling are encouraged to apply. Please contact us via email if you are interested in joining.

Graduate Students

Prospective graduate students should directly apply to UTSW Graduate Programs. If you are a recently recruited graduate student and interested in our work, please personally visit our lab and learn about the projects for rotation students.
